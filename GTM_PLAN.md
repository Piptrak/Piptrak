# PIPTRAK — Go-To-Market Plan
# Strategy to acquire first 1,000 paying users

---

## TARGET CUSTOMER

**Primary:** Retail forex trader attempting a prop firm challenge
- Age 22–40, predominantly male
- Spending $100–$600 on challenge fees
- Losing money on challenges due to poor rule discipline
- Active on Reddit, Twitter/X, YouTube, Discord, TikTok

**Secondary:** Funded traders managing multiple accounts
- Already passed at least one challenge
- Looking for portfolio-level visibility

---

## PHASE 1 — FIRST 100 USERS (Month 1–2)
Goal: Validate product, collect testimonials, get first revenue

### Week 1–2: Reddit Launch
- Post to r/Forex, r/Forexnoobs, r/propfirms, r/Daytrading
- Title formula: "I kept failing FTMO challenges until I built this tracker — sharing it free"
- Show real dashboard screenshot. Show the daily loss alert feature.
- Offer FREE Pro for first 50 signups (collect emails, get feedback)
- Engage every comment personally

### Week 3–4: Twitter/X Content
- Post daily content: prop firm tips, rule reminders, trade setups
- Tweet your own Piptrak dashboard daily (show the product in use)
- Use hashtags: #propfirm #FTMO #forextrader #fundedtrader
- DM micro-influencers (1K–10K followers) — offer free Pro in exchange for honest review post

### Month 2: YouTube Shorts & TikTok
- "Watch me pass an FTMO challenge using Piptrak" series
- Short-form: "The #1 reason traders fail prop firm challenges" → Piptrak solution
- "Day X of my $100K FTMO challenge" daily series

---

## PHASE 2 — FIRST 500 USERS (Month 2–4)
Goal: Build a community, get affiliate partners

### Discord Community
- Create "Piptrak Traders" Discord server
- Free members get access, Pro members get private channels
- Post daily market commentary, share challenge progress
- Community creates organic retention and word-of-mouth

### Affiliate Program
- 30% recurring commission for affiliates
- Target: forex YouTubers, Twitter traders, Discord server owners
- Give each affiliate a unique link and dashboard to track signups
- Top affiliates: offer 40% + co-marketing opportunities

### Content SEO
- Blog posts targeting: "how to pass FTMO", "FTMO rules explained",
  "best prop firm challenge tracker", "MT4 trade journal"
- These rank for high-intent keywords from traders researching

### Prop Firm Partnerships
- Reach out to smaller prop firms to white-label or recommend Piptrak
- Offer revenue share for referrals from their user base

---

## PHASE 3 — FIRST 1,000 USERS (Month 4–6)
Goal: Paid acquisition becomes profitable

### Paid Ads (start small: $10/day)
- Meta Ads: target "forex trading", "prop firm", "FTMO" interests
- YouTube pre-roll on forex content channels
- Creative: "Failed 3 FTMO challenges? This is why." → Piptrak

### Email Marketing
- Weekly newsletter: prop firm tips, trader spotlights, product updates
- Onboarding sequence (see email-sequences/ folder)
- Winback sequence for churned Pro users
