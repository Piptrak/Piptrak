# NexusAI — Your Launch Checklist
Everything you need to do to go live and start earning.

---

## STEP 1 — Upload to Netlify (FREE, 5 minutes)
1. Go to netlify.com → Sign up free
2. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag & drop your index.html file
4. Your site is LIVE at a random URL like: https://happy-tree-123.netlify.app

---

## STEP 2 — Buy a Domain (~$10/year)
1. Go to namecheap.com
2. Search "nexusai" or your preferred name + .com
3. Buy it
4. In Netlify: Site settings → Domain management → Add custom domain
5. Follow Netlify's instructions to connect it (takes ~10 min)

---

## STEP 3 — Set Up Google Analytics (FREE)
1. Go to analytics.google.com → Create account
2. Create a Property → get your Measurement ID (looks like G-XXXXXXXXXX)
3. Open index.html → find "G-XXXXXXXXXX" (appears twice) → replace with your ID
4. Re-upload to Netlify

---

## STEP 4 — Set Up Email List with Mailchimp (FREE up to 500 subscribers)
1. Go to mailchimp.com → Sign up free
2. Create an Audience
3. Go to: Audience → Signup forms → Embedded forms
4. Copy the "form action" URL
5. Open index.html → find "handleEmailSignup()" function
6. Replace the TODO comment with a fetch() call to your Mailchimp URL
   OR just forward to a Mailchimp hosted signup page

---

---

## STEP 6 — Set Up Stripe (for paid plans)
1. Go to stripe.com → Sign up (need bank account)
2. Go to: Payment Links → Create a link for $29/month
3. Copy the link → paste it into index.html where it says "Start free trial"
4. Do the same for $99/month Business plan

---

## STEP 7 — Start Writing Blog Posts (drives free traffic)
Best first posts to write:
- "Best AI tools for [your niche] in 2026"
- "ChatGPT vs Claude — Which is Better for Business?"
- "10 AI tools under $20/month that are worth it"
- "How I saved 10 hours/week using AI tools"

Post them on: Reddit (r/artificial, r/entrepreneur), LinkedIn, X/Twitter

---

## WHAT TO CHANGE IN index.html
Search for these placeholders and update them:
- G-XXXXXXXXXX → Your Google Analytics ID
- hello@nexusai.com → Your real email
- href="#" on tool cards → Your affiliate links  
- href="#" on pricing buttons → Your Stripe payment links
- The "48,000+ readers" stat → Update as you grow
