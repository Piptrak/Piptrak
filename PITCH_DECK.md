## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

**$2.3 billion is spent on prop firm challenge fees every year.**

The failure rate is staggering:
- **78% of traders fail** their first prop firm challenge
- Most failures happen not from bad trading — but from **breaching a daily loss or drawdown rule**
- Traders lose $200–$600 per failed attempt and retry repeatedly
- There is no dedicated tool to help them track their rules in real time

> "I passed the challenge on my 4th attempt. The first 3 I blew on emotional trading days when I lost track of my daily limit."
> — Common story in every prop firm community

---

## SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION

**Piptrak is a mobile-first challenge tracker and trade analytics platform.**

- 📊 Real-time dashboard showing challenge progress vs rules
- ⚡ Breach alerts via push, email, and SMS before limits are hit
- 🔗 Automatic trade sync from MT4/MT5 brokers
- 📈 Deep analytics: profit factor, best pairs, day-of-week P&L
- 💼 Multi-account management across all prop firms

**One sentence:** Piptrak is the tool that sits between a trader and their funded account.
