🚀 Getting Started
How do I add my first challenge account? ▼
Go to the Accounts tab in the bottom navigation → tap + New Account → select your prop firm (FTMO, Funded Next, The5ers etc.) → enter your account size and challenge start date. Your dashboard will automatically load the correct rules for that firm.
Which prop firms does Piptrak support? ▼
Piptrak has pre-loaded rules for FTMO, Funded Next, The5ers, and MyFundedFX. You can also choose Custom and enter your own profit target, daily loss limit, and max drawdown percentages. We add new firms regularly — contact us to request one.
How do I log a trade manually? ▼
Go to the Trades tab → tap + Log Trade → fill in the pair, direction (BUY/SELL), lot size, entry/exit price, P&L amount, date, and optional notes. Your dashboard updates instantly. For automatic logging, use CSV import or broker sync.
Can I track multiple challenges at the same time? ▼
Yes. Free plan supports 1 account, Pro supports up to 5, and Elite supports unlimited. Switch between accounts using the chips at the top of the dashboard. Each account has its own trade history, P&L, and rules tracking.
Will my data be saved if I close the app? ▼
Yes. All your trades, journal entries, and settings are saved to your account in our database. You can access your data from any device by logging in at app.piptrak.com.
🔗 Broker Sync
How do I import trades from MT4/MT5 via CSV? ▼
In MT4: go to Account History tab → right-click → Save as Report → choose CSV. In Piptrak, go to Trades tab → tap the CSV button → select your file. Piptrak auto-detects columns for Symbol, Type, Lots, Open/Close Price, Profit, and Date.
How does the MetaApi live sync work? ▼
MetaApi is a third-party service that connects to your MT4/MT5 broker. Sign up free at metaapi.cloud → add your broker account → copy your API token and Account ID into Piptrak's Broker tab. Your trades will sync automatically. Requires Pro or Elite plan.
What is the webhook and how do I use it? ▼
The webhook is a URL your MT4/MT5 EA (Expert Advisor) can POST to every time a trade closes. Go to the Broker tab → copy your unique webhook URL and secret → paste the EA code snippet into MetaEditor. Each trade will appear in Piptrak within seconds of closing. Requires Elite plan.
Will duplicate trades be imported twice? ▼
No. Piptrak uses your broker's trade ticket number as a unique ID. If you import the same trade twice (via CSV or webhook), only one copy is saved. You can safely re-import your full history without creating duplicates.
🔔 Alerts & Notifications
How do I set up breach alerts? ▼
Go to the Alerts tab → toggle on the alerts you want (Daily Loss Warning, Max Drawdown, Target Hit) → choose your delivery method (push, email, or SMS). SMS requires a verified phone number. Alerts require a Pro or Elite plan.
At what threshold do daily loss alerts fire? ▼
You'll receive warnings when your daily loss reaches 70% and 90% of your limit. For example, on an FTMO $10,000 account (5% daily loss = $500 limit), you'll be alerted at $350 and $450 of daily loss. This gives you time to stop before a breach.
I'm not receiving push notifications — what should I do? ▼
Check that: (1) notifications are enabled for your browser in device Settings, (2) you've toggled Push on in Piptrak's Alerts tab, (3) you haven't blocked notifications for piptrak.com. Try refreshing the app and re-enabling notifications.
💳 Billing & Plans
How does the 7-day free trial work? ▼
When you start a Pro trial, no payment is taken. After 7 days, your card is charged $15 for the first month. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends in Settings → Billing — you will never be charged. One free trial per account.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
Go to Settings → Billing → Manage subscription. This opens the Stripe billing portal where you can cancel, change plan, or update your payment method. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep access until then.
Can I get a refund? ▼
We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first payment if you're not satisfied. After 7 days, refunds are at our discretion. Contact support@piptrak.com and we'll do our best to help.
Is there a discount for annual billing? ▼
Yes — annual billing saves you 25%. Pro annual is $135/year ($11.25/month) vs $15/month on the monthly plan. Elite annual is $351/year ($29.25/month) vs $39/month. Switch in Settings → Billing → Change plan.
👤 Account & Security
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to piptrak.com/reset-password → enter your email → check your inbox for a reset link. The link expires in 1 hour. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete account. This permanently deletes all your data including trades, journal entries, and account settings. This action cannot be undone. Your subscription will be cancelled automatically.
Is my trading data secure? ▼
Yes. Your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Row-level security means only you can access your own data — even our team cannot see your trades. Broker API tokens are stored encrypted. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
