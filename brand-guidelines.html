Logo Do's & Don'ts

Use on dark backgrounds (#090c12 or similar)
Maintain clear space equal to hex width around logo
Use SVG format for all digital uses
Use light variant on white/light backgrounds only
Stretch or distort the logo proportions
Change the logo colours outside the brand palette
Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
Add drop shadows, outlines or effects to the logo
