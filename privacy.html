Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2025

Piptrak ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at piptrak.com and our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Trading data: Trade history, P&L figures, account sizes, and challenge progress that you enter or import.
  • Broker connection data: MetaApi tokens and MT4/MT5 account identifiers (stored encrypted).
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never store your card details.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration — collected anonymously for analytics.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, IP address for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Piptrak service
  • To sync your broker trades and display your challenge progress
  • To send breach alerts and notifications you have opted into
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To respond to support requests and communications
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted on AWS), protected by:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • Broker tokens encrypted before storage
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing only
  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure
  • MetaApi — only if you connect a broker account
  • Twilio — only if you enable SMS alerts

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@piptrak.com.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings but this may affect functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Piptrak is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or in-app notification. Continued use of Piptrak after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions: privacy@piptrak.com
For general support: support@piptrak.com
